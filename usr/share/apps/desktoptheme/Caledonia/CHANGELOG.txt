(...LEGACY VERSIONS...)

Caledonia Plasma Theme had first release in Jun 05 2011 (based in many parts of "Ember" theme by Garth Johnson), and changes the license (move to Creative Commons) 
in Oct 14 2011 (version 0.9.8) because of many new and remade components.
Only versions prior to 0.9.8 keeps the GPL license. See the metadata or the changelog to know the Caledonia version.
